About Adjacent Science

Our Mission

Adjacent Science exists to make the hidden wiring of science visible. We believe that better data and smarter analytics can accelerate discovery, improve funding decisions, and drive technological progress for the benefit of society.

Leadership

Ben Johnson

Adjacent Science is led by Ben Johnson. Ben is Professor of Practice in Research and Innovation Policy at the University of Strathclyde and heads the science policy work of the Centre for British Progress. From 2019-2024, Ben was senior policy adviser to the UK Minister of State for Universities and Science and later the Secretary of State for Science, Innovation and Technology. He has held a number of roles in both the civil service and in UK research funding agencies since 2009.

Our Approach

We combine cutting-edge network analysis, policy expertise, and deep understanding of the research landscape to deliver actionable insights. Our work spans from detailed analytics for R&D policymakers to strategic intelligence for research leaders, always focused on implementation-ready solutions that drive real progress.
